Sun Gym Management System

Primary Tables

  • Members - Manage member profiles, view and update membership details
  • Trainers - Manage trainer profiles and their specializations
  • Classes - Manage fitness classes, schedules, and capacity
  • Equipment - Track gym equipment inventory and maintenance status
  • Payments - Track and manage member payments

Relationship Management

  • Class Bookings - Manage member class enrollments and attendance
  • Equipment Usage - Track and manage member equipment usage
  • Trainer Equipment - Manage trainer equipment certifications and assignments
  • Member-Trainer Assignments - Manage personal training relationships and schedules
